Best Brownie's Recipe

These are my favorite brownies. I hope that you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsn butter
  • 2 tbsn water
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup all purpose flower
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Grease an 8-in square pan.
  2. Preheat the ovn to 325 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Combine sugar, butter, and water in a medium saucepan. Cook over medium heat and stir until boiling.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until smooth.
  5. Mix in eggs and vanilla.
  6. Combine flour, salt, baking soda and stir into chocolate mixture.
  7. Spread batter evenly into prepared pan.
  8. Bake for 20-30 minutes, until top is dry and edges start to pull away from sides of the pan. Let cool completely before cutting.
